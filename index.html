About QCE

We treat every home like it is on inspection.

QCE started in Vancouver with one rule. No job leaves until it passes the same check an inspector would run. That is where the name comes from. Quality control is the work, not a slogan on the van.

We focus on residential electrical for houses, strata units, and laneway homes across the Lower Mainland. Alongside that, we take small institutional troubleshooting for schools, clinics, and offices that need a fault found fast.

The crew is licensed electricians who pull permits, label their work, and clean up before they leave. You get a clear quote in CAD, a tested result, and a warranty that means something.

No upsells on work you do not need. We tell you what is safe, what is urgent, and what can wait, then we let you decide.
